What I can help with

These are small, focused tasks — not full redesigns or large projects.

  • Fixing small broken or weird-looking parts of your existing website (buttons, links, layout, spacing).
  • Setting up a simple contact form that sends messages directly to your email inbox.
  • Tiny layout or text updates so your site looks a bit cleaner and easier to read.

How it works

A simple, message-only process designed for busy people.

  1. 1. You send a short message

    Tell me what’s broken or what feels off — and share a link to your website.

  2. 2. I take a look and suggest a fix

    I check what’s possible, come back with a simple explanation and what I can do. If it looks good to you — I start.

  3. 3. You review the result

    I show you the change, you test it, and we adjust if anything still feels off. Once you’re happy — we’re done.
